List of Exercises:

    1. Stretch cerv sidebends arm outstretched

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Stand or sit.
    • Reach downward with arm.
    • Place other arm on top of head as shown.
    • Bend head while pulling down gently to stretch muscles on opposite side of neck.
    • Repeat to other side.
    • Perform 1 set of 3 repetition(s), Tu, Th, Sa.
    • Hold exercise for 30 Seconds.

    2. Stretch Rhomboids stand at door

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Hold onto door jamb or other rigid support with both hands.
    • Keep elbows straight and lean back as shown, until a gentle stretch is felt between shoulder blades.
    • Perform 1 set of 3 repetition(s), Tu, Th, Sa.
    • Hold exercise for 30 Seconds.

    3. Stretch Pectoral standing w/shld ER

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Stand at doorway with forearms on door frame, elbows bent to 90 degrees.
    • Keep back straight and step forward with one leg until a gentle stretch is felt across chest and in front of shoulders.
    • Perform 1 set of 3 repetition(s), Tu, Th, Sa.
    • Hold exercise for 30 Seconds.

    4. Stretch thoracic sidebend sit

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Sit toward front of chair so that arms can hang in line with front chair legs.
    • Keep feet and knees wide to help maintain balance.
    • Place left hand on left side of head.
    • Lean to right, sliding right hand down the right leg of chair.
    • Return to start position and repeat.
    Special Instructions:
     Repeat exercise to other direction if so instructed.
    • Perform 1 set of 3 repetition(s), Tu, Th, Sa.
    • Hold exercise for 30 Seconds.

    5. Stretch hip/knee figure 4

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Lie on back, knees bent.
    • Move left ankle over right knee.
    • Gently lift right knee up to chest until stretch is felt.
    • Repeat with other leg.
    • Perform 1 set of 3 repetition(s), Tu, Th, Sa.
    • Hold exercise for 30 Seconds.

    6. Resist shld ER uni stand (abd 45) w/elastic

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Attach elastic to secure object at waist level.
    • Place pillow between elbow and body.
    • Grasp elastic in hand, elbow bent to 90.
    • Rotate arm outward and return.
    • Slowly return to start position and repeat.
    • Perform 3 sets of 10 repetition(s), Tu, Th.
    • Use Elastic.
    • Rest 1 Minute between sets.
    • Perform 1 repetition every 4 Seconds.

    7. Resist shld IR uni w/elastic

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Secure elastic at waist level.
    • Sit or stand with involved side to elastic, elbow at 90, arm at side.
    • Grasp elastic and pull hand inward, across body, as shown.
    • Slowly return to start position and repeat.
    Special Instructions:
     Keep arm at side.
    • Perform 3 sets of 10 repetition(s), Tu, Th.
    • Use Elastic.
    • Rest 1 Minute between sets.
    • Perform 1 repetition every 4 Seconds.

    8. Resist shld bent row w/elastic

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Secure elastic under opposite foot.
    • Hold elastic in involved arm.
    • Slightly bend hips and knees and support upper body with other arm as shown.
    • Pull up on elastic, raising elbow to shoulder height.
    • Slowly return to start position and repeat.
    Special Instructions:
     Contract abdominal muscles and maintain a neutral spine, not allowing trunk to twist.
    • Perform 3 sets of 10 repetition(s), Tu, Th.
    • Use Elastic.
    • Rest 1 Minute between sets.
    • Perform 1 repetition every 4 Seconds.

    9. Resist shld upright rows bil w/elastic

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Stand on elastic.
    • Grasp elastic in both hands in front of hips, elbows straight.
    • Lift upward toward chin, bending elbows.
    • Keep hands close to chest.
    • Slowly lower and repeat.
    • Perform 3 sets of 10 repetition(s), Tu, Th.
    • Use Elastic.
    • Rest 1 Minute between sets.
    • Perform 1 repetition every 4 Seconds.

    10. Resist shld Clock Exercise Diag

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Hold elastic in both hands.
    • Visualize a clock where 12:00 is to above and 6:00 in toward the floor.
    • Pull elastic to 3 and 9.
    • Pull elastic to 1 and 7.
    • Pull elastic to 11 and 5.
    • Pull elastic to 12 and 6.
    • Switch lead arm and repeat.
    • Perform 1 set of 10 repetition(s), Tu, Th.
    • Use Elastic.
    • Rest 1 Minute between sets.
    • Perform 1 repetition every 4 Seconds.

    11. Resist shld wall walk w/elastic

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Loop elastic around forearms.
    • Stand a few feet from a wall.
    • Place hands on wall.
    • Walk with hands sideways across wall.
    • Then walk with hands up and down wall.
    • Perform 1 set of 10 repetition(s), Tu, Th.
    • Use Elastic.
    • Rest 1 Minute between sets.
    • Perform 1 repetition every 4 Seconds.

    12. Resist shld abd bil (vert emphasis) w/elastic

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Stand on elastic.
    • Hold elastic in both hands.
    • Begin with arms at sides.
    • Position palms forward.
    • Keep elbows straight and lift arms to shoulder level.
    • Slowly lower and repeat.
    • Perform 3 sets of 10 repetition(s), Tu, Th.
    • Use Elastic.
    • Rest 1 Minute between sets.
    • Perform 1 repetition every 4 Seconds.

    13. Resist elbow flx bil w/wt

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Begin with arms at side, sit or stand, elbow straight, palms up, weights in hand.
    • Bend elbows upward.
    • Return to starting position.
    Special Instructions:
     Keep elbows close to sides through entire movement.
    • Perform 3 sets of 10 repetition(s), Sa.
    • Use Lbs.
    • Rest 1 Minute between sets.
    • Perform 1 repetition every 4 Seconds.

    14. Resist elbow ext overhead stand w/wt

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Hold weight in hand.
    • Position arm overhead, elbow bent, as shown.
    • Straighten arm.
    • Return to start position and repeat.
    • Perform 3 sets of 10 repetition(s), Sa.
    • Use Lbs.
    • Rest 1 Minute between sets.
    • Perform 1 repetition every 4 Seconds.

    15. Resist shld bent row partial kneel w/wt

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Begin in position shown with arm supported on knee.
    • Keep back straight, parallel to floor, with shoulders squared.
    • Begin with weight on floor.
    • Raise it up to abdomen, keeping elbow close to side.
    • Lower and repeat.
    • Perform 3 sets of 10 repetition(s), Sa.
    • Use Lbs.
    • Rest 1 Minute between sets.
    • Perform 1 repetition every 4 Seconds.

    16. Resist shld ER prone w/wt

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Lie face down.
    • Hold weight in hand.
    • Place arm at 90 degrees, elbow bent, as shown.
    • Rotate arm upward, keeping elbow bent.
    • Perform 3 sets of 10 repetition(s), Sa.
    • Use Lbs.
    • Rest 1 Minute between sets.
    • Perform 1 repetition every 4 Seconds.

    17. Resist shld chest press palm down w/wt.

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Lie on back on weight bench
    • Hold weights in hands, palms down, arms out to sides, elbows bent to 90.
    • Lift weights up and to front of body while straightening arms as shown.
    • Return and repeat.
    • Perform 3 sets of 10 repetition(s), Sa.
    • Use Lbs.
    • Rest 1 Minute between sets.
    • Perform 1 repetition every 4 Seconds.

    18. Resist chest press palm in w/wt.

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Lie on back on weight bench
    • Hold weights in hands, arms out to sides, elbows bent to 90.
    • Lift weights up and to front of body while straightening arms as shown.
    • Return and repeat.
    • Perform 3 sets of 10 repetition(s), Sa.
    • Use Lbs.
    • Rest 1 Minute between sets.
    • Perform 1 repetition every 4 Seconds.

    19. Dynam hip flx/ext standing

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Begin standing with left side to wall, leaning on wall with left hand.
    • Swing right leg forward and backward.
    • Repeat.
    • Repeat series with right side to wall and swinging left leg.
    • Perform 1 set of 5 repetition(s), F.
    • Perform 1 repetition every 4 Seconds.

    20. Dynam knee circle fwd

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Lift leg outward from body while bending knee.
    • Circle thigh forward to front of body and lower, returning to standing.
    • Repeat series with other leg
    • Perform 1 set of 5 repetition(s), F.
    • Perform 1 repetition every 4 Seconds.

    21. Dynam hip flx stand (knee to chest)

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Begin standing.
    • Lift knee to chest, and use arms to pull to chest.
    • Lower leg.
    • Repeat with other leg.
    • Perform 1 set of 5 repetition(s), F.
    • Perform 1 repetition every 4 Seconds.

    22. Dynam hip diag D1 stand

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Begin standing. Lift leg, bending knee and bringing foot upward toward hips.
    • Grab ankle with hands and lift leg a bit higher.
    • Lower leg and repeat.
    • Repeat series with other leg.
    • Perform 1 set of 5 repetition(s), F.
    • Perform 1 repetition every 4 Seconds.

    23. Dynam hip abd/flx sidelying

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Begin lying on side. Lift top leg, bending knee and bringing foot in front of hips.
    • Return and repeat. Repeat series lying on other side.
    • Perform 1 set of 5 repetition(s), F.
    • Perform 1 repetition every 4 Seconds.

    24. AROM lumbar alt leg/arm (bird dog)

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Begin on hands and knees.
    • Extend the right leg while lifting the left arm.
    • Hold 2-3 seconds.
    • Return to start position and repeat with left leg and right arm.
    Special Instructions:
     Maintain neutral spine, do not twist.<br />Progress by placing weights on ankles and wrists.
    • Perform 2 sets of 20 repetition(s), F.

    25. AROM lumbar bridging w/alt legs

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Lie on back with knees bent and feet flat on floor.
    • Maintain neutral spine.
    • Lift buttocks off floor.
    • Hold position and alternate lifting legs up as shown.
    • Lower and repeat.
    • Perform 2 sets of 20 repetition(s), F.

    26. AROM hip/knee flx unsupported w/alt arms supine

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Lie on back with legs straight, low back in neutral position.
    • Raise right leg and left arm off floor.
    • Slowly straighten right leg and lower left arm, while bringing left leg and right arm up.
    • Alternate as shown.
    • Arms and legs do not return to floor.
    Special Instructions:
     Maintain neutral spine without twisting or rotating hips. Move in smooth and controlled movements.
    • Perform 2 sets of 20 repetition(s), F.

    27. AROM lumbar half kneel to quadruped

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Begin in half kneel position with arms over head as shown.
    • Maintain neutral spine by tightening abdominal muscles.
    • Slowly lower arms and reach forward to floor.
    • Return to start position and repeat.
    • Perform 2 sets of 20 repetition(s), F.

    28. Dynam knee lunge fwd w/trunk rotn

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Begin standing.
    • Step forward with right leg into a deep lunge while twisting body to left.
    • Step back up to standing and repeat.
    • Repeat series stepping forward with left leg and twisting right.
    • Perform 1 set of 10 repetition(s), F.
    • Perform 1 repetition every 4 Seconds.

    29. Dynam knee lunge bkwd diag w/trunk rotn

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Begin standing.
    • Step backward 45 degrees to right with right leg into a deep lunge while twisting body to right.
    • Step back up to standing and repeat.
    • Repeat series stepping backward with left leg and twisting to left.
    • Perform 1 set of 10 repetition(s), F.
    • Perform 1 repetition every 4 Seconds.

    30. Dynam leg side lunge squat

    Exercise Instructions:
    • Begin standing.
    • Step to right, dropping hips into a deep squat, keeping shin vertical and upper body forward as shown.
    • Bring left leg toward right leg and step up towards right into standing.
    • Repeat side walk lunging.
    • Perform 1 set of 10 repetition(s), F.
    • Perform 1 repetition every 4 Seconds.
